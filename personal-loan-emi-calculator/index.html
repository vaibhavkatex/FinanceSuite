Personal Loan FAQ

A Personal Loan is an unsecured loan offered by financial institutions for personal expenses such as medical emergencies, home renovation, wedding, travel, or debt consolidation. Since it requires no collateral, interest rates are higher compared to secured loans.

In addition to interest rates, lenders charge:

  • Processing Fees: 1% to 3% of the loan amount.
  • Preclosure/Foreclosure charges: 2% to 5% if paid before tenure completion.
  • Late payment charges: Penalties for missed monthly dates.
