PPF Account Benefits & Rules

Public Provident Fund (PPF) is a popular long-term savings scheme backed by the Government of India. It offers attractive interest rates and returns that are fully exempt from tax, making it a highly secure retirement planning option for retail investors.

PPF enjoys the coveted Exempt-Exempt-Exempt (EEE) tax status:

  • Exempt 1: Deposits up to ₹1.5 Lakhs per annum qualify for deductions under Section 80C.
  • Exempt 2: Annual interest accrued is fully tax-free.
  • Exempt 3: The entire maturity corpus is completely exempt from income tax at withdrawal.
